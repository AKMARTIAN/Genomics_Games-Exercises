# Dubai Police Genome Center — Forensic Genomics Activities

Welcome! These are four hands-on exercises that put you in the seat of a forensic genomicist at the **Genome Center, Department of Forensic Science and Criminology**.

## How to use them

- Open an activity in any web browser. It runs in the browser and works offline — only the BARCODE *Live BLAST* version needs internet.
- Pick the **case number** your instructor gives you; the class works the same case.
- Work **as a group**, agree on an answer, then press **“Show our group's answers.”**
- Report your answer to your instructor — they hold the key and will tell you how you did.

> On the web, open **`index.html`** for a friendlier welcome page.

## The activities

### IRIS — DNA Phenotyping
You're handed an unidentified blood sample. Read the DNA sequencing traces at six positions and work out what the donor looks like — eye, hair and skin colour.

**Open:** [`IRIS_DNA_Phenotyping_STUDENT.html`](IRIS_DNA_Phenotyping_STUDENT.html)

### VERDICT — Clinical Variant Interpretation
Act as the clinical bioinformatician: find the variant in a patient's gene, work out its effect, look it up in a ClinVar-style database, and decide what it means for the patient.

**Open:** [`VERDICT_clinical_variants_STUDENT.html`](VERDICT_clinical_variants_STUDENT.html)

### ORIGIN — Biogeographic Ancestry
An unidentified person's DNA has gaps. Fill them in by imputation, then read the ADMIXTURE and PCA plots to infer which part of the world they're from.

**Open:** [`ORIGIN_ancestry_STUDENT.html`](ORIGIN_ancestry_STUDENT.html)

### BARCODE — Species Identification
Identify the species behind a mystery sample by DNA barcoding — spot ivory, food fraud, contamination and more. A self-contained simulator, plus a live version that uses the real NCBI BLAST website.

**Open:** [`BARCODE_species_id_STUDENT.html`](BARCODE_species_id_STUDENT.html) · **Live BLAST version:** [`BARCODE_live_BLAST_STUDENT.html`](BARCODE_live_BLAST_STUDENT.html)
